How the app works

  • Swipe the button up once a day to record your day. One dot lights up.
  • Swipe it back down before midnight to undo. The day is locked at midnight.
  • A cycle lasts one year from your first record, then the field resets.
  • Reset in the menu clears all dots and starts over.
