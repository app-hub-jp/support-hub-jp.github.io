Privacy Policy

IN. does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

  • All records you create in the app are stored only on your device and in the local shared storage used by the widget.
  • The app has no accounts, no analytics, no ads, and no third-party services.
  • The app makes no network connections. Your data never leaves your device.
  • If you delete the app, all of its data is deleted with it.

Because no data is collected, there is nothing for us to sell, share, or lose.

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us via the Support page.
