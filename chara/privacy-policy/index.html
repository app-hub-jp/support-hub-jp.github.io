Privacy Policy

English

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use CHARA.

Effective date: July 14, 2026

Eligibility

You must be at least 18 years old to use CHARA. CHARA is not directed to people under 18. If you provide information about another person, including a child in your care, you confirm that you are authorized to do so.

Information we process

When you request a name, CHARA processes the information you enter: nationality, gender, age, height, weight, personality answers, and app language. CHARA does not require an account, your real name, or your email address to generate a name.

For service operation and abuse prevention, we also process an app-generated installation identifier, request and response status, processing time, model and generation settings, prompts, and AI responses. Service providers may process technical information such as IP address, device or advertising identifiers, network information, and timestamps.

If you contact us, we process your email address and the information included in your message to respond to you.

How we use information

We use information to generate Japanese names, display results, operate credits and rewarded ads, prevent abuse, diagnose errors, improve generation quality, respond to support requests, comply with law, and protect the service.

AI name generation and consent

The profile information, personality answers, and app language you provide are sent through our Supabase-hosted service to Google Gemini. CHARA asks for your explicit consent before the first generation.

We retain the final prompt sent to Gemini, Gemini's unprocessed response, model and generation settings, result status, error information, and processing time for up to 30 days for quality and error investigation. The final prompt may contain the profile and personality information used for generation.

Google's Gemini API terms distinguish between paid and unpaid services. Under paid-service terms, Google states that prompts and responses are not used to improve its products, although they may be retained for a limited period for abuse monitoring, security, legal, or regulatory purposes. If a request is processed under unpaid-service terms, Google may use submitted content and generated responses to improve its products, and human reviewers may process them. See the Gemini API Additional Terms and Google Privacy Policy.

Advertising and privacy choices

CHARA uses Google Mobile Ads to offer optional rewarded advertising. Google and its partners may process device and advertising identifiers, IP address, interactions with ads, approximate location derived from IP address, and consent choices, depending on your region and device settings.

Where required, CHARA displays Google's consent form before requesting ads. When available for your region, you can reopen Ad Privacy Options from the app menu. Learn more at How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services.

Purchases

Credit purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive your payment card or bank account details. CHARA stores a credit balance and identifiers for processed App Store transactions on your device. Apple processes purchase and account information under the Apple Privacy Policy.

Information stored on your device

CHARA stores app preferences, AI consent status, advertising consent information managed by Google's SDK, an app-generated installation identifier, credit balance, and processed transaction identifiers on your device. The installation identifier is sent to our service for rate limiting and is stored there only as a one-way hash.

Service providers and international processing

We use Supabase for backend hosting and database services, Google Gemini for AI name generation, Google Mobile Ads for rewarded advertising and consent management, and Apple for distribution and in-app purchases. These providers may process information in countries other than where you live, subject to their terms, privacy policies, and safeguards.

Retention and security

Generation diagnostic logs are retained for up to 30 days. Hashed rate-limit records are retained for up to 8 days. Information stored locally remains until removed by the app, the operating system, or deletion of the app. Support emails are retained only as reasonably necessary to respond, keep support records, resolve disputes, and comply with law.

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information, but no internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed completely secure.

Your choices and requests

You may decline AI processing by not continuing past the consent screen. You may later select Revoke AI Consent in the app menu. Revocation applies to future transmissions and does not automatically delete information already processed or stored.

You may request access, correction, or deletion by emailing supporthubjp+chara@gmail.com. Because CHARA has no user accounts and stores only a hashed installation identifier for rate limiting, we may not be able to identify a specific record without enough information from you.

Changes and contact

We may update this policy when CHARA, our providers, or applicable requirements change. We will post the updated policy here with a revised effective date.

Email: supporthubjp+chara@gmail.com
